Our Vision And The Transformation
Learn how one family restaurant brought San Jos√© together for 60 years. When Rashmi's Bakery opened in 1946, it offered a smorgasbord, barbecued dinner, and dancing in its 5,500 square foot building. For decades, Rashmi's Bakery was known for offering live entertainment in addition to quality food. Over its long and colorful history, millions of guests enjoyed entertainment, parties and fine food at Rashmi's Bakery. Many of our staff have been with us for a number of years and have become part of our extended family. One of our staffs still comes by to see old customers and friends; she turned 91 in July 2016.

Our Vision

Rashmi's Bakery is a beacon of light for an alternative way of living to achieve World Peace. While striving to make Our Vision a reality, we continually focus on improving our Core Values.

Our Core Values

HEALTHY: Serve delicious meals using only natural and healthy ingredients.
COMPASSION: Honor all lives to live in peace & harmony together.
GREEN: Respect & protect Mother Earth through sustainable practices.
SERVICE: Treat each customer as a VIP, a friend and a partner.
HAPPINESS: Provide joyful experiences for customers, employees, friends, and families.
LOYALTY: Make Rashmi's Bakery a destination of choice for all.
